LOGO PLACEHOLDER

Your Rustborne logo goes here!

Location: y:\Rustborne\frontend\public\logo.png

Recommended specifications:
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Size: 512x512 pixels or larger
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- Color: Works best with the rust-orange (#e04d1a) theme

How to add your logo:
1. Save your logo as "logo.png" in the public folder
2. The header will automatically display it next to "RUSTBORNE"
3. If no logo is found, the text-only header displays

If you want a custom size or positioning, edit the Header.tsx component's img tag.
